Organizational Controls

Organizational controls in ISO/IEC 27001 include policies, roles, and processes used to maintain information security within an organization.

 

Examples of Organizational Controls

 

  • Establishing information security policies
  • Assigning roles and responsibilities
  • Conducting security awareness training
  • Incident management processes
  • Regular internal audits

 

Benefits of Control Implementation

 

  • Increases security awareness across the organization
  • Helps with compliance to standards and regulations
  • Reduces the risk of data breaches
  • Increases trust from partners and customers

Implementing proper organizational controls creates a strong foundation for information security management and helps organizations meet their security objectives while maintaining compliance with relevant standards.
